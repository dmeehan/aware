AWARE Institute

Animal Welfare Assessment, Research and Education: Using Science to Understand Animal Welfare

What We Offer

  • Information & Tools

    The goal of our work is to support animal care professionals with information and tools to enable enhanced animal care.

  • Proven Expertise

    We offer a unique combination of animal welfare science expertise, advanced software tools and superior collaborative project management.

  • Actionable Data

    Our projects are designed to augment the art of animal care with high quality data.

  • Informed Decision Making

    Knowledge helps you enhance your practice. Data can support informed decision making and resource allocation, and common metrics can help programs demonstrate success and identify opportunities for improvement.
